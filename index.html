// About

Who We Are

The NKU Data Science Club provides a forum for NKU students to develop hands-on skills in data science and analysis. Our community consists of faculty and students at Northern Kentucky University.

We host various events including workshops, hackathons, and industry speaker sessions. Check out some upcoming events below!
