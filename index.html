About Us

The NKU Data Science Club provides a forum for NKU students to develop hands-on skills in data science and analysis. The DSC community consists of faculty and students at Northern Kentucky University.

The Data Science Club hosts various events including workshops, hackathons, and industry speakers. Check out some upcoming events below!

3-Step-Eye-Detection

Our three-model approach enhances road safety by detecting driver drowsiness in real-time. The system activates when the car is running, checks for a driver, and analyzes eye features to identify signs of drowsiness. If the driver appears to be sleeping, it triggers alerts like intermittent braking or honking to prevent accidents. Stay safe on the road!
